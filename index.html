Frequently Asked Questions

To check price history, install the **edy** browser extension. When you visit a product page on supported e-commerce sites (like Amazon or Flipkart), a price history graph will automatically appear on the page, showing price fluctuations over time.

Price History is a graphical representation of how the cost of a specific product has changed over a period (e.g., 30 days, 6 months, or 1 year). This helps you determine if the current price is a good deal or if you should wait for a drop.

A Price Tracker, such as the one offered by **edy**, allows you to set a target price for a product. Once the price drops to or below your desired threshold, the tracker automatically sends you an alert (via email or notification), ensuring you never miss a deal.

A Price Drop Alert is a notification generated by the **edy** system when the price of a tracked item falls significantly, often below its average historical price or below a price you manually specified. It's an instant notification designed to prompt a purchase.

Edy helps you save money by providing real-time price tracking, historical price data, and personalized alerts for price drops on products from supported stores like Amazon and Flipkart. By analyzing trends and discounts, it ensures you buy at the best possible time, avoiding overpriced purchases and maximizing savings on everyday items.
